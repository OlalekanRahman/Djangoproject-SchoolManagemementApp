KCGS At a Glance

This is Koso Community Grammar School, Iseyin, founded in 1976 and located along Iseyin-OdoOgun road, Iseyin, Oyo state.
The current Principal is Mr. Mukaila Popoola and the current VPs are Masters Arasi and Adefabi and a female VP Jokanola(Mrs).

KCGS's Administrative Hierarchy

KCGS maintains administrative hierarchy in the order below:
  • The Pricipal
  • The Vice Pricipal(Administration)
  • The Vice Pricipals(Academic)
  • The Senior Teaching Staff
  • The Junior Teaching Staff
  • The Non-Teaching Staff Rank and File
